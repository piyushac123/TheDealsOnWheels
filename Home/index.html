HYDRO-GRAPHIC PRINTING

It is a method of applying printed designs to three-dimensional surfaces. The Hydro-Graphic Printing process can be used on metal, plastic, glass, hard woods, and various other materials.

One of the hottest paint finishes currently around is Chrome. The Chrome Finish can be used on wheel rims , mirror covers, car-door handles , helmets and accessories.

QUALITY CAR DETAILING

Normal wear-and-tear on road will do a number on interior & exterior of your vehicle. We perform top quality detailing services that will have your vehicle sparkling inside and out.
