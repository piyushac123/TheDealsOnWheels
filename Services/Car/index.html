CAR DETAILING


Car Detailing

Our full service auto repair shop is here to provide you vehicle’s cosmetic enhancements. Normal wear-and-tear on the road will do a number on the interior and exterior of your vehicle. Vehicles not only look better, but they drive better after they’ve been through a car wash. But a simple wash is not enough for a lot of vehicles.
The Deals on Wheels performs top quality auto detailing services that will have your vehicle sparkling inside and out. For any good detailing service it’s all about the finer details. We use the industry’s best detailing equipment to clean all surfaces using compressed air, tiny brushes, and special cleaning solutions. The foot traffic in your vehicle will accumulate dirt, debris, and cause carpet wear. Our top-notch vacuuming and shampoo equipment is able to remove all traces of grime from your carpet. Every inch of your vehicle’s interior–including under the seat, the dashboard, cup holders, center console, vents, and more–is cleaned to match our exceptional standards.
